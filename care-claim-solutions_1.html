How it works

A four-step cycle built for clean submissions the first time.

Step 1

Intake & verification

We receive the episode documentation and confirm payer eligibility before coding begins.

Step 2

Coding & review

Diagnosis and OASIS coding is completed and cross-checked against documentation.

Step 3

Clean claim submission

The claim is submitted through the appropriate clearinghouse or payer portal.

Step 4

Follow-up to payment

We track the claim to resolution, appealing where needed until it's paid in full.
